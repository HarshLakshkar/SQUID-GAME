Black Market is a game where players must choose a shape from a set of shapes. The player who chooses the shape that is not in the set

Red Light Green Light

It is a children's game, where players must stop when the leader says "red light" and go when the leader says "green light".

Cookie Cutter

Cookie Cutter is a game where players must use a needle to extract a shape from a sugar cookie without breaking the cookie.

Tug of War

Tug of War is a sport that directly puts two teams against each other in a test of strength: teams pull on opposite ends of a rope, with the goal being to bring the other team across a central line.

Marbles

Marbles is a game played with small, round glass balls called marbles. The object of the game is to collect as many marbles as possible by shooting your marble into a circle of marbles.

Stepping Stones

Stepping Stones is a game where players must cross a bridge made of glass panels. Some of the panels are tempered and some are not, and players must choose the correct panels to step on.
